About Us

Yashoda Tele-Health Services include a range of remote consultation services that can facilitate critical care with the help of specialists to remote locations, smaller hospitals, and nursing homes. The Yashoda Tele-Health services include Tele-Medicine, Tele-ICU, Tele-Pathology, Tele-Education, Tele-Physiotherapy, Tele-Dietetics, and Tele-Radiology.
